Welcome to the new nteract blog.

This space is now part of the main site and will be where we share more about
the product, upcoming releases, technical notes, and the thinking behind the
work we are doing.

## What to expect

Over time, this blog will become the home for:

- release announcements
- product updates
- technical writeups
- notes on workflows for humans and agents

## Why this lives in the repo

We wanted publishing to stay simple and low-overhead. Posts live alongside the
site code, which makes writing, reviewing, and deploying new content feel just
like the rest of the project.

That means new writing can be drafted in pull requests, reviewed in context, and
deployed with the same workflow as everything else on nteract.io.

## More soon

This is only the beginning, but it gives the site a clear home for longer-form
updates.

More content will be posted here soon.
