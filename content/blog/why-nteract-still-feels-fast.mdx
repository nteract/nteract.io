Speed on a marketing page usually means page load. Speed in a notebook product
is broader than that.

## Fast to launch

The quickest workflows are the ones that do not ask for much ceremony. nteract
opens directly into work instead of making the interface feel like a control
panel you have to configure before you can think.

## Focused by default

A lot of the product experience comes from what *is not* on the screen:

1. fewer competing controls
2. readable default spacing
3. a layout that prioritizes cells and outputs over chrome

That same philosophy is useful on the website too. The blog keeps the reading
experience quiet and lets the content carry the page.

## Built for people and agents

The product line on the home page still captures the idea well:

> Fast to launch. Agent ready. Humans welcome.

We want the surrounding site and the writing to reinforce that same feeling.
